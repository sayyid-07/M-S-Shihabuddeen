Namma Vastra - AI Powered Handloom Marketplace & Trend Guide

Project Overview Namma Vastra is an Android-based digital marketplace and specialized trend analysis platform developed using Kotlin and Jetpack Compose in Android Studio.

The project is designed to revitalize traditional weaving ecosystems, bridge the gap between rural handloom artisans and global consumers, and promote cultural preservation by leveraging contemporary Artificial Intelligence (GenAI) and Modern Mobile Architecture.

Objectives

Eliminate Middlemen Exploitation: Empower authentic weavers to sell directly to premium markets, maximizing their profit margins.
Modernize Traditional Design Pipelines: Provide rural artisans with data-driven style insights to keep their weaves competitive in contemporary global markets.
Combat Counterfeits: Protect consumers and the integrity of the handloom industry through instant digital material validation.
Promote Sustainable Fashion: Drive conscious consumerism by highlighting zero-carbon footprint, handmade textiles.
Foster Cultural Tourism: Use interactive mapping to highlight the stories, geography, and distinct identity of regional weaving hubs.
Features

📱 Android Application Architecture

Developed using native Kotlin and built from the ground up in Android Studio.
Designed with Jetpack Compose and Material 3, featuring a fluid, dynamic user interface with cultural color palettes.
Built adhering to MVVM (Model-View-ViewModel) design patterns and Clean Architecture principles for high performance and scalability.
🛍️ Direct-to-Consumer (D2C) Marketplace

Dedicated storefront enabling weavers to seamlessly catalog, list, and price their unique handmade textiles.
Unified secure onboarding system for independent creators and local cooperative societies. 🔮 Predictive AI Trend Engine
Powered by the Google Gemini API to scan global textile markets, fashion datasets, and seasonal color palettes.
Translates complex analytics into simple visual design suggestions (motifs, popular cuts, hot colorways) for artisans.
📸 Smart Fabric Verification

Implements on-device camera capabilities coupled with computer vision models to assist buyers in detecting real weave spacing and identifying authentic handloom textiles.
🗺️ Artisan Story & Heritage Maps

Interactive geographical map integration showcasing regional weaving clusters (e.g., Kanjeevaram, Ikat, Ilkal) and detailing their distinct histories.
💬 Multilingual Partner Portal

Voice-to-text navigation utilities and comprehensive localized language support tailored for varied literacy levels among rural partners.
